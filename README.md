## Project Overview
The Customer Feedback & Sentiment Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze customer reviews, ratings, complaints, and service performance. The dashboard helps businesses understand customer satisfaction, identify areas for improvement, and monitor product and platform performance through visual analytics.

## Project Objectives
- Analyze customer feedback across different product categories.
- Measure customer satisfaction using ratings and sentiment.
- Track complaint registrations and issue resolution.
- Monitor average response time for customer support.
- Enable interactive filtering using slicers.

## Project Insights
Based on the analysis of the Customer Feedback & Sentiment Analysis Dashboard, the following insights can be derived:
- Customer Sentiment
  - Positive feedback represents satisfied customers and highlights strengths in product quality and service.
  - Negative feedback indicates areas requiring immediate attention to improve customer experience.
- Product Category Performance
  -Some product categories receive consistently higher customer ratings, while others require quality improvements and better customer support.
- Response Time
  - Faster response times are generally associated with better customer satisfaction, whereas delayed responses often lead to negative feedback and complaints.
- Issue Resolution
  - A high issue resolution rate reflects efficient customer support and contributes to improved customer trust and loyalty.
- Regional Trends
  - Complaint levels vary across regions, helping businesses identify locations that need operational improvements or enhanced customer service.

## Final Conclusion
- The Customer Feedback & Sentiment Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer satisfaction, service quality, and operational performance. By monitoring key metrics such as customer ratings, sentiment, response time, complaint registrations, and issue resolution, businesses can identify strengths, address customer concerns, and make informed decisions.
- The interactive dashboard enables stakeholders to explore data by region, product category, platform, and customer demographics, making it easier to uncover trends and prioritize improvements. Overall, this project demonstrates how data visualization can transform customer feedback into actionable business insights, helping organizations improve customer experience, increase satisfaction, and support data-driven decision-making.
